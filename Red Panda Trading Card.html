Red Panda

Red Pandas are not related to Giant Pandas.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus Fulgens
  • Average Length: 20-25 Inches Long
  • Life Span: Captive: 9-13 Years; Wildllife: Unknown
  • Habitat: Temperate Forest with a Thick Bamboo Understory

    • Red Pandas have large, round heads and short snouts with big, pointed ears. Their faces are white with reddish-brown “tear” marks that extend from the eyes to the corner of the mouth. These markings could have evolved to help keep the sun out of their eyes. Their tails are marked with alternating red and buff rings.
